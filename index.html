When the past keeps showing up,
you don’t have to face it alone.

EMDR helps the brain process difficult memories so they feel less overwhelming.

EMDR can help if you:

  • Relive painful or intrusive memories
  • Feel stuck in the past
  • Live with constant worry or low mood
  • Feel on edge or overwhelmed
  • Avoid things because of fear or panic
  • Feel trapped by phobias

About EMDR at Strega Health

EMDR is an evidence-based therapy that helps the brain process memories that feel stuck. Strega Health offers trauma-informed EMDR therapy in a calm, supportive setting.
